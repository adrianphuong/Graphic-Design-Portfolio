How do I order?

You can either add me on Discord: adrianrblx or DM me on Twitter.

How can I determine the cost of my logo?

The price of the logo depends on the complexity and the time in which you need it completed. No I do not do same day delivery or 1 day delivery unless compensated fairly.

What I should I prepare before I contact you?

Here's a list of what I need from you:
1.) Name of your logo
2.) What the logo is for (Game/Studio)
3.) Any references that you like
4.) Color Palette/Color Choice wanted
5.) Illustrations wanted
6.) And finally the deadline

Is that all?

Unfortunately, no.
- My policy is that I require 50% of the payment upfront.
- You also must pay for any taxes/fees that arise when completing the deal.
- Lastly there are no refunds whatsoever.
I accept Paypal, Cashapp, Venmo, and Robux.
